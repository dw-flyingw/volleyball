Legal alignment
    The overlap rules (at moment of serve)
    Front row = 4 / 3 / 2 (nearest net). Back row = 5 / 6 / 1.

    Left–right order
    Front: 4 left of 3, 3 left of 2.
    Back: 5 left of 6, 6 left of 1.

    Front behind back
    Each front player must be closer to the net than the back player behind them: 2 ahead of 1, 3 ahead of 6, 4 ahead of 5.

    The server (position 1) is exempt from overlap once the ball is contacted, but this trainer checks the pre-serve line-up.
